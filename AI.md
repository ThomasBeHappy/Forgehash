# AI assistance notice

Parts of this project were developed with help from AI coding assistants
(for scaffolding, packaging, docs wiring, and similar chores). That help was
**assistive**, not a substitute for human design: the algorithms, specs,
vectors, and core implementations were directed and reviewed by a human, and
the bulk of the research write-ups were written by hand.

AI also helped with drafting or editing some research-facing material
(including notes and the ForgeHash-X paper pipeline). Those documents should
still be read as **human-authored research notes**, not as AI-generated
claims or proofs. Numbers and campaign results come from running the tooling
in this repo — verify anything that matters before you rely on it.

Nothing here is certified, peer-reviewed in the academic sense, or suitable
for production password storage.
